About Our Foundation

Empowering Nigeria's future through education, opportunity, and community development.

Our Mission

The Foundation envisages a future where free educational institutions are established, and health services provided free of charge to the less privileged and financially disadvantaged people in our society. We believe that every life is important, and education is the bedrock of a nation. So, everyone, including the poor, should have the privilege to access health care and the formal education they desire and need.

Our Vision

The Augustine Udefa Foundation is committed to improving the lives of people, especially those with the fewest resources, by providing opportunities for them to access free education and quality health services.

Foundation Bylaws

The governing principles and operational guidelines of the Augustine Udefa Foundation.

Article I: Name and Purpose

The name of this organization shall be the Augustine Udefa Foundation (AUF), a nonprofit organization established under the laws of Nigeria.

The purpose of the Foundation is to provide educational opportunities to underprivileged students in Nigeria through scholarships, infrastructure development, and community programs.

Article II: Governance

The Foundation shall be governed by a Board of Trustees consisting of not fewer than five (5) and not more than nine (9) members. Trustees shall serve three-year terms with the possibility of renewal.

The Board shall meet quarterly and is responsible for setting policy, approving budgets, and ensuring the Foundation fulfills its mission.

Article III: Financial Management

All funds received by the Foundation shall be used exclusively for charitable and educational purposes as outlined in the mission statement.

The Foundation shall maintain complete financial records and undergo annual independent audits. No part of the net earnings shall benefit any private individual.

Article IV: Scholarship Awards

Scholarships shall be awarded based on financial need, academic merit, and leadership potential without regard to ethnicity, religion, or gender.

The Scholarship Committee shall establish transparent selection criteria and procedures, with all decisions being final.
