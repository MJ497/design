To apply for our scholarships:

  1. Register for an account on our scholarship portal
  2. Complete your profile with academic records and personal information
  3. Select the scholarship you wish to apply for
  4. Upload required documents (transcripts, recommendation letters, etc.)
  5. Submit your application before the deadline

You can track your application status through your portal account.

Eligibility varies by scholarship program, but general requirements include:

  • Nigerian citizenship or permanent residency
  • Proof of financial need (for need-based scholarships)
  • Minimum academic performance (varies by program)
  • Admission or enrollment in an accredited institution
  • Completion of all application requirements

Specific criteria for each scholarship are listed on the program details page.

Communities can benefit through:

  • Nominating your community for our quarterly medical outreach programs
  • Requesting malaria prevention education and net distribution
  • Partnering with us for school health initiatives
  • Participating in our maternal health workshops

Contact our health programs coordinator at health@auf.org.ng to discuss opportunities.

Augustine Udefa Speaking Scholarship

The Augustine Udefa Speaking Scholarship is awarded to students in Nigerian educational institutions, who can demonstrate good public speaking skills and critical thinking ability. The scholarship is awarded to students in any discipline, and winning it is contingent on the student being of good moral conduct and winning a series of speaking competitions. The scholarship provides full tuition support for the duration of your studies and a one-time cash payment.

Eligibility

  • You must be a student in a Nigerian public university, polytechnic, monotechnic, or college of education in your first or second year. However, you may be considered if you're in your third or fourth year and you can demonstrate that you are passing through financial hardship.
  • You must be of good moral conduct.
  • You must submit all required application documents via the application portal. Documents required include curriculum vitae, admission letter, and an attestation letter from your Head of Department (HOD) on their letterhead, attesting to your good moral conduct.

Scholarship Value

  • Winner: Full tuition fee support for the period of your program. Cash prize of N100,000.
  • 2nd Position: Full tuition fee support for one academic session. Cash prize of N50,000.
  • 3rd Position: Full tuition fee support for one academic session.

Renewal & Termination

  • Renewal requires a minimum CGPA and attestation letters.
  • Termination for poor conduct or not meeting requirements.

How to Apply

  • Click “Apply” and complete the application form, uploading all required documents.
  • Required documents: Cover letter, CV, admission letter, attestation letter from HOD.

Speaking Competition

The competition will be held in January 2026 on our social media channels. Stages include group, semi-final, and final rounds. Winners are selected by judges and public vote.

For more information, contact info@augustineudefafoundation.com

Augustine Udefa Orphan Scholarship

The Augustine Udefa Orphan Scholarship is awarded to people who have lost both parents and do not have any sponsor to support their education. Successful applicants are those who, without the scholarship, would be unable to acquire an education. This scholarship is awarded to applicants in both secondary and tertiary institutions in Nigeria.

Eligibility

  • You have lost both of your parents and do not have any sponsor.
  • You have successfully gained admission into or are in a public tertiary institution in Nigeria if you intend to acquire tertiary education.
  • You are in a public secondary school in good academic standing and can no longer continue your education because of the demise of your parents.

Scholarship Value

  • Full tuition fee throughout your program.

Application Requirements

  • Admission letter
  • Curriculum vitae
  • Personal statement (500–1000 words), explaining your situation.
  • Academic report from your school, showing your grades and other necessary information.
  • Three letters of recommendation, confirming that you have lost both of your parents and can no longer continue your education. Two should come from religious leaders and one from your teacher.
  • An attestation letter from your head of department or school authority, confirming that you have been admitted into or are currently enrolled in your school.

Renewal & Termination

  • Renewal requires a minimum CGPA and attestation letters.
  • Termination for poor conduct or not meeting requirements.

How to Apply

  • Click “Apply” and complete the application form, uploading all required documents.

Augustine Udefa Excellence Scholarship

The Augustine Udefa Excellence Scholarship is a highly competitive scholarship awarded to students with excellent academic profile. Successful applicants for this scholarship are high-scoring students who have demonstrated academic excellence at all levels.

Eligibility

  • Be a student in a Nigerian public university or polytechnic in your second or third year.
  • Have a minimum cumulative grade point average (CGPA) of 4.30 on a 5.0 scale or 3.30 on a 4.0 scale.
  • Be of good moral conduct.

Scholarship Value

  • Full tuition fee throughout your program.

Application Requirements

  • Admission letter
  • Curriculum vitae
  • Personal statement (500–1000 words), introducing yourself and detailing your academic achievements and awards.
  • Unofficial student transcript or academic report, showing courses taken, grades obtained, and CGPA.
  • Three letters of recommendation from academic referees.
  • Attestation letter from your head of department or school authority, confirming your status as a student in your institution.

Renewal & Termination

  • Renewal requires a minimum CGPA and attestation letters.
  • Termination for poor conduct or not meeting requirements.
  • Termination for misconduct or promoting nudity/sexual immorality on social media.

How to Apply

  • Click “Apply” and complete the application form, uploading all required documents.
