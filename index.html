About the Foundation

Our Vision

Founded in 2012 by philanthropist Augustine Udefa, our foundation is dedicated to creating educational opportunities for underprivileged students across Nigeria. We believe education is the most powerful tool for breaking the cycle of poverty.

To date, we've awarded over 1,200 scholarships, built 3 school libraries, and provided essential learning materials to 15,000+ students in rural communities.

Augustine Udefa

Founder & Chairman

Augustine Udefa is a visionary entrepreneur and philanthropist who founded the Augustine Udefa Foundation in 2012. With over 30 years of business experience as the former CEO of Udefa Holdings, he has dedicated the past decade to transforming education in Nigeria.

Born in a rural village in Enugu State, Augustine understands firsthand the transformative power of education. Despite humble beginnings, he earned a scholarship to study Business Administration at the University of Lagos, which launched his successful career.

Key Achievements:

  • Founded AUF in 2012 with initial personal investment of ₦500 million
  • Recipient of the National Honor of Member of the Order of the Niger (MON)
  • Named "Philanthropist of the Year" by ThisDay Newspaper in 2018
  • Established 3 fully equipped school libraries in rural communities

Dr. Amina Okeke

Education Director

Dr. Amina Okeke brings over 25 years of academic excellence to her role as Education Director. As a Professor of Education at the University of Lagos, she specializes in curriculum development and educational policy.

With a PhD in Educational Psychology from the University of Ibadan, Dr. Okeke has published numerous papers on effective learning methodologies in African contexts. She joined the AUF board in 2015 and has since revolutionized our scholarship selection criteria.

Key Contributions:

  • Developed AUF's merit-based scholarship assessment framework
  • Pioneered the foundation's teacher training programs
  • Led the design of 3 STEM-focused curricula for rural schools
  • Recipient of the Nigerian Academy of Education blue Medal (2019)

Chioma Eze

Finance Director

Chioma Eze is a Fellow of the Institute of Chartered Accountants of Nigeria (ICAN) with 18 years of financial management experience. She oversees all financial operations of the foundation, ensuring transparency and accountability.

Before joining AUF in 2016, Chioma served as CFO for a leading Nigerian bank, where she implemented innovative financial strategies. Her expertise has helped the foundation maximize the impact of every donated naira.

Financial Milestones:

  • Reduced administrative costs by 35% while increasing program funding
  • Implemented blockchain-based donation tracking system
  • Maintained 100% clean audit reports since joining the foundation
  • Spearheaded endowment fund that now stands at ₦2.3 billion

Emeka Nwankwo

Programs Director

Emeka Nwankwo is a social entrepreneur who has dedicated his career to youth development. As Programs Director since 2014, he oversees all scholarship distributions, mentorship programs, and community initiatives.

With degrees in Sociology and Public Administration, Emeka brings a unique perspective to educational access. His "Whole Child" approach ensures scholarship recipients receive holistic support beyond just tuition payments.

Program Innovations:

  • Created AUF's award-winning mentorship program
  • Expanded scholarship reach to 12 additional states
  • Developed the "Digital Literacy for All" initiative
  • Implemented alumni tracking system with 92% engagement rate
